## 简单的工程描述

    target("console")
        set_kind("binary")
        add_files("src/*.c") 

## 构建工程

    xmake

## 运行目标

    xmake run console

## 支持特性

* Tasks
* Macros
* Actions
* Options
* Plugins
* Templates

## 支持平台

* Windows (x86, x64, amd64, x86_amd64)
* Macosx (i386, x86_64)
* Linux (i386, x86_64, cross-toolchains ...)
* Android (armv5te, armv6, armv7-a, armv8-a, arm64-v8a)
* iPhoneOS (armv7, armv7s, arm64, i386, x86_64)
* WatchOS (armv7k, i386)
* Mingw (i386, x86_64)

## 支持语言

* C/C++
* Objc/Objc++
* Swift
* Assembly
